Fausto Corrêa da Silva (São Paulo, May 2, 1950), popularly known as Faustão, is a Brazilian presenter, comedian and broadcaster, Since January 17, 2022, he presents the program Faustão na Band, on TV Bandeirantes. With a vast career in radio that began in the 1960s, focused especially on sports publishing, Fausto debuted on television in 1984 with the auditorium program Perdidos na Noite on TV Gazeta, which later went to TV Record and TV Bandeirantes. His biggest success was the auditorium program Domingão do Faustão, shown uninterruptedly by TV Globo from 1989 to 2021, being one of the longest-running programs on Brazilian television.
